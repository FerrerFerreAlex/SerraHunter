Hashin-Shtrikman bounds

Hashin-Shtrikman bounds


  • Shear Modulus

    \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \mu_{\small{LB}}(\rho)=\mu_{0}(1-\rho)+\mu_{1}\rho-\frac{(1-\rho)\rho(\mu_{1}-\mu_{0})^{2}}{\mu_{0}\rho+\mu_{1}(1-\rho)+\frac{\kappa_{0}\mu_{0}}{2\mu_{0}+\kappa_{0}}} \\ \mu_{\small{UB}}(\rho)=\mu_{0}(1-\rho)+\mu_{1}\rho-\frac{(1-\rho)\rho(\mu_{1}-\mu_{0})^{2}}{\mu_{0}\rho+\mu_{1}(1-\rho)+\frac{\kappa_{1}\mu_{1}}{2\mu_{1}+\kappa_{1}}} \end{aligned} \end{equation}
  • Bulk Modulus

    \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \kappa_{\small{LB}}(\rho)=\kappa_{0}(1-\rho)+\kappa_{1}\rho-\frac{(1-\rho)\rho(\kappa_{1}-\kappa_{0})^{2}}{\kappa_{0}\rho+\kappa_{1}(1-\rho)+\mu_{0}} \\ \kappa_{\small{UB}}(\rho)=\kappa_{0}(1-\rho)+\kappa_{1}\rho-\frac{(1-\rho)\rho(\kappa_{1}-\kappa_{0})^{2}}{\kappa_{0}\rho+\kappa_{1}(1-\rho)+\mu_{1}}. \end{aligned} \end{equation}
