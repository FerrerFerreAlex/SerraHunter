First result

Topological Derivative and
H-S bounds connexion

  • Limit of H-S bound interpolation function

    \begin{equation} \begin{array}{c} g_{\small{LB}}(0) = -\nabla^{s}u:\mathbb{C}_{\small{LB}}'(0):\nabla^{s}u \quad \text{with} \quad \mathbb{C}_{\small{LB}}(\rho) = 2\mu_{\small{LB}}(\rho)\mathbb{I}+[{\kappa_{\small{LB}}(\rho)-\mu_{\small{LB}}(\rho)}]I\otimes I \\ \\ % \quad \text{and} \quad g_{\small{UB}}(1) = -\nabla^{s}u:\mathbb{C}_{\small{UB}}'(1):\nabla^{s}u \quad \text{with} \quad \mathbb{C}_{\small{UB}}(\rho) = 2\mu_{\small{UB}}(\rho)\mathbb{I}+[{\kappa_{\small{UB}}(\rho)-\mu_{\small{UB}}(\rho)}]I\otimes I. \end{array} \end{equation}

  • TD coincides with H-S bounds limits

    \begin{equation} \begin{cases} \mathbb{C}_{\small{LB}}'(0) = d\mathbb{C}_{0} & \text{when} \ \tilde{x}\in \Omega_{0} \\ \mathbb{C}_{\small{UB}}'(1) = d\mathbb{C}_{1} & \text{when} \ \tilde{x}\in \Omega_{1} \end{cases} \quad \text{thus} \quad \begin{cases} g_{\small{LB}}(0) = g_{T_0}(\tilde{x}) & \text{when} \ \tilde{x}\in \Omega_{0} \\ g_{\small{UB}}(1) = g_{T_1}(\tilde{x}) & \text{when} \ \tilde{x}\in \Omega_{1}. \end{cases} \end{equation}

  • TD can be interpreted as inserting (in the limit) HS microstructures.
