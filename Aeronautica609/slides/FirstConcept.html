First concept

Topological derivative

  • Sensitivity of a functional when:
    • adding a small bubble: $\Omega \mapsto \Omega_{x,r} := \Omega \cup B(x,r)$ for $x \notin \overline{\Omega}$
    • nucleating a small hole: $\Omega \mapsto \Omega_{x,r} := \Omega \setminus\overline{B(x,r)}$ for $x \in \Omega$


  • Definition: A functional $F(\Omega)$ has a topological derivative at $x \in \Omega^0 \cup \Omega^1$ if there exist the following asymptotic expansion
    $$F(\Omega_{r,x}) = F(\Omega) + s_{\Omega}(x) \color{blue}{g_{\Omega}^T(x)} |B(x,r)| + o(|B(x,r)|).$$
    where $s_\Omega(x)=1$ when $x\in \Omega^0$ and $s_\Omega(x)=-1$ when $x\in \Omega^1$.

Topological derivative

  • Isotropic topological derivative for the compliance:

    $g_\Omega^T = \left \{ \begin{split} - e(u_\Omega):d\mathbb{C}_0:e(u_\Omega) & \quad \text{if} \in \Omega^0 \\ - e(u_\Omega):d\mathbb{C}_1:e(u_\Omega) & \quad \text{if} \in \Omega^1 \end{split} \right. $
    with

    $ \begin{split} d\mathbb{C}_0 = 2 d\mu_0 \mathbb{I} + (d\kappa_0 - d\mu_0 ) I\otimes I \\ d\mathbb{C}_1 = 2 d\mu_1 \mathbb{I} + (d\kappa_1 - d\mu_1 ) I\otimes I \end{split} $
    where

    \begin{equation} {{d\mu}_{0}} = \mu_{0}(\mu_{1} - \mu_{0})q_{{\mu}_{0}} \text{,} \quad {q_{{\mu}_{0}}} = \frac{(\mu_{0}+{\eta_{\mu}}_0)}{\mu_{0}(\mu_{1}+{\eta_{\mu}}_0)} \quad \text{and} \quad {\eta_{\mu}}_0 = \frac{\kappa_{0}\mu_{0}}{2\mu_{0}+\kappa_{0}} \end{equation}

    \begin{equation} {{d\kappa}_{0}} = \kappa_{m}(\kappa_{I} - \kappa_{m})q_{{\kappa}_{0}} \text{,} \quad {q_{{\kappa}_{0}}} = \frac{(\kappa_{0}+\mu_{0})}{\kappa_{0}(\kappa_{1}+\mu_{0})} \end{equation}
